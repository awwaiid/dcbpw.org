    • Where to Stay

    There are many hotels in the area, we have highlighted several below. Since a lot of attendees are local, there might be some couch opportunities. You are generally on your own there, but feel free to use the wiki to coordinate and/or contact the organizers if you have any trouble finding a place to stay!

    Courtyard by Marriott on Fenton Street

    NOTE: Our reserve price expires April 4, 2014!

    This is our recommended hotel, located in downtown Silver Spring, MD. Follow this link to get our group rate of $125 for King/ $135 for Doubles. The rate is secured until April 4th.

    You can also call 1-800-321-2211 for a reservation. Let the reservation specialist know that you want to stay at the Courtyard Silver Spring Downtown hotel (on Fenton Street). Ask for the DCBPW (DC-Baltimore Perl Workshop) room block.

    8506 Fenton Street
    Silver Spring, MD 20910

    DoubleTree by Hilton

    Also nearby and also good, the DoubleTree is located just a few blocks (easily walkable) from the venue.

    8727 Colesville Road Silver
    Spring, MD 20910
